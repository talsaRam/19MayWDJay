Welcome To Aesthetics by Jennifer

At Aesthetics By Jennifer, we offer a range of aesthetic services to help you feel confident and look your best. From botox injections to facials and nails, we have the treatment for you!

About Us

Jennifer, a proud Navy veteran and UF Alumnus began her career in aesthetics with renowned plastic surgeon Dr. Richard Gregory, here in her hometown of Orlando, and later worked for Dr. Eliot Battle in our Nation's Capitol where she learned to treat skin of all colors. Over her 16 years in practice, she has continually honed and refined her skills, staying current with the latest innovations through ongoing training to better serve her. clients. Jennifer excels as a gifted injector and practitioner, performing treatments that include Botox, Dysport, Dermal fillers, PDO Lifting Threads as well as Wellness services such as IV Hydration and B12 shots. Home skincare as well as supplemental products are always welcomed to discuss to enhance what is done in the office. We at Aesthetics by Jennifer look forward to meeting and working with you soon!
