About FCSI

Forensic Cleaning Services Ireland was formed by Brendan Reilly and Micheal Holahan in 2011.

Prior to establishing their own company Brendan and Micheal had previously spent over 10 years working in the fire and flood damage restoration industry in Ireland.

They decided to diversify when they identified a gap in the Irish market for a specialist cleaning and restoration firm.

They gained the required qualifications and training needed to deal effectively with the most difficult and demanding aspects of their jobs.

For instance, some of the tasks that they can undertake include; crime scene clean up, body fluid removal, decomposition restoration. Hypodermic needle collection, removal of contaminated flooring, deodorizing, chemical and equipment purchasing. In addition, they also offer full health and safety risk assessments.

Crime Scene Clean-Ups

Crime scene clean-ups involve painstaking work to detect and disinfect. FCSI completely remove all traces of bio-hazardous materials. Removal of blood, bodily fluids, tissue and bone matter, contaminated clothing and household goods. Similarly, the disposal of hypodermic needles, razor blades and other items that pose a risk of injury or infection.

It is a job that demands compassion and sensitivity when working with crime survivors and family members affected by the tragic situation.

We strive to remove the heartache and pain of having to clean up these situations regardless of the condition of the body or the location.

Service

FCSI offer a wide range of services from sanitisation to property clearance. These specialist areas combined with FCSI's core work in fire and flood damage restoration leaves it in an excellent position to offer a diverse range of services to our clients.

Forensic Cleaning Services Ireland provides specialist cleaning and restoration services covering all requirements from water and flood damage to fire and smoke damage, crime scene cleanup to death scene cleanup.
