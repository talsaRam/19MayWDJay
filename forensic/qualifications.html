Qualifications

To gain qualifications FCSI founders ,Brendan and Michael have both completed an extensive course at the National Academy of Crime Scene Cleaners.

National Academy of Crime Scene Cleaners

This group of experts came together to raise the standard of the industry. In doing so, they created unique solutions for customers’ facing difficult circumstances. The result is the increasing professionalism of the crime scene cleaning service. Clients now realising that in order to perform their responsibilities properly, they are required to use professional operatives. Qualified individuals like Brendan and Michael who have been fully trained on an approved training scheme. Individuals who have received the knowledge and knowhow to help customers in special fields. As a result, Forensic Cleaning Services Ireland is now affiliated to the National Academy of Crime Scene Cleaners. The NACSC is the recognized awarding body of our industry in Ireland. Therefore, it is a mark of quality and reassurance.

Qualifications with Quality of Service

Both Brendan and Michael are fully qualified and insured to carry out all bio-hazardous jobs. Like any sector of the industry, we pride ourselves on the level of work we do. The standard and quality of service are highlighted by the number of clients who return to us time and time again. When we say no job is too large or too small, we mean it. From minor smoke damage restoration to the cleanup of a complete building. We have and will manage it all. This makes our services ideal for every type of customer on a once-off or contract basis. As a team, we are both professional and discrete at all times. Our services are available 24/7 nationwide. We never forget that even the most minor of projects for us is a major inconvenience and cause of stress to you. Do take the time to look through our testimonials from the many customers who have used our services in the past. These will help to underline the value of our services.
