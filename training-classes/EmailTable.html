Back Splash
A Few tips to keep in mind, when choosing a back splash...
  • When choosing a kitchen Back splash, keep in mind that the
    back splash should complement your counter tops and cabinets. Picking a busy or patterned back splash can even date your kitchen wih the year of the "trend".
  • We suggest choosing a simple, neutral colored tile. Because it is simple, chances are you will not tire of it easily. It is better for resale, and you can easily change up the look of your kitchen, with simple pops of color, using smaller items, i.e.: towels, rugs, vases, etc.
  • The classic subway tile, in a neutral tone will always be in style and you will get your monies worth.
